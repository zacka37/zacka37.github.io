Zack Augustine - Personal Reflection

Starting college at the University of Missouri (Mizzou) was at first confusing because I did not know what I wanted to do for a lifelong career even after 2 years at a community college. I started Mizzou with an Arts and Science major with emphasis in film production. With that major you had to take a Spanish class as well as many films history and film production classes.

After my first semester at Mizzou, I then talked to a guidance counselor and decided to change my major to Information Technology. I am so happy that I changed my major early at Mizzou and not near the end of graduation. Changing majors has helped my GPA tremendously because I am very interested and highly involved in all my Information Technology classes.

I will be graduating the School of Engineering at Mizzou in December of 2022. I will be graduating with a BS in Information technology and 4 Certifications. I would have graduated in the Spring of 2022, but I changed my major. Even though changing my major added another semester of school, I am glad that I made the switch and am happier taking IT classes than film production classes.

After I graduate, my goal is to have a job lined up for me to start immediately. My dream job is to be a software engineering at a FANG company. FANG is an abbreviation for Facebook, Apple, Netflix, and Google. I took many Cyber Security classes at Mizzou, but I enjoy software engineering more. I enjoy software engineering mainly because of the creative freedom that is possible and the ever-evolving technology that is used.
